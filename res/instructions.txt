PRODUCT LINE:

-Enter the Product's name in the "Product Name" TextField.

-Enter the Product's manufacturer in the "Manufacturer" TextField.

-Select the appropriate ItemType for the Product.

-Press the "Add Product" button after filling in all of the necessary information.

-The Product that you entered will be listed in the TableView.

PRODUCE:

-The Product you entered will also be listed in the ListView in this tab.

-Select a Product from the ListView. You can also search for a product by name,
manufacturer, and type.

-To search for a product, select the appropriate filter for the product you are
searching for.

-If you wish to search for all products, enter nothing in the TextField and press search.

-Once a product has been selected, choose the quantity amount you wish to produce.

-The "Quantity Amount" ChoiceBox contains choices 1-10.
You can also enter your own quantity.

-Once all of the necessary information has been entered, press "Record Production".

-The item will be recorded to the Production Log, and text will appear in the
TextArea displaying the item you have made.

PRODUCTION LOG:

-In this tab, you will see all of the items that you have produced.

-The Item is listed by Production number, Product name,
Serial Number, and Date produced.

EMPLOYEE:

-Enter your first and last name in the "Employee Name" TextField.

-Enter a password for the "Password" TextField.

-The password must contain an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, and a special
character.

-Once the necessary information has been entered, press the "Submit" button.

-Your employee details will be displayed in the TextArea below.
